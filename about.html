About this Website

Hello! I'm Alina (or "Yuki Yuna"), I'm a big fan of cozy games and especially Planet Zoo.
I also like visiting zoos (and other animal related places) in almost every place I travel to if possible!
I started this site as a project for a front end course I was taking and thought it would be fun to make something game related.

I took some inspiration from other fansites and spreadsheets made by the community, thank you to everyone!
